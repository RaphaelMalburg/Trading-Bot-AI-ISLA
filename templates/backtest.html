Notes & Caveats

  • Backtest assumes immediate fills at the close price — real-world slippage will reduce returns slightly.
  • Sentiment scores are not backtested — they only veto trades in live mode where headlines are available.
  • Past performance never guarantees future results, especially in crypto.
  • The bot runs on Alpaca paper trading by design; this is a university assessment, not a production strategy.
